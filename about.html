About me

I guess I should introduce myself better. I am 35 years old, I am married, we live in Lviv, Ukraine. In 2021, I decided to change my career. Originally, I have a higher education in Hospitality Management, which I didn't particularly enjoy. After working for a couple of years, I ditched that job, and went pursuing my creative goals. Since childhood, I played on various musical instruments, like guitar, accordion, drums, then learned to play some piano. Wrote music for more than 10 years in total. Did freelance jobs, worked with various music agencies and libraries.

Eventually it became clear that this field has lost it's magic for me. So I went trying everything that was hot at that time. I did vector graphics for a year, managed to get into stock agencies and sell my vector art. Learned 3D modelling, started selling some models. Learned animation and motion graphics, made a couple of projects, but something was off. My spouse recommended me to look into programming, so I did.

After learning some basics and playing around with Python and Java, I joined a Full Stack Web Development course. Closer to an end of the course I decided to focus on Front End. I like everything about it. The possibilities are endless. The industry is blooming, each new skill I learn just adds up to what I already know. It is like an ever-growing tree, which one may climb his whole life. Anyway, since it is almost a year since I started my learning, I feel the need to work with other people, on real projects. If, by any chance, you are looking for a new developer, please consider me as a good option. I like to learn and I like to do stuff, the only thing I want now is to develop with other people. I would be glad to work on-site, but I am open to anything. Thanks!
